Description:

"It's 3:33AM, also known as Dr3:AM, and the player must navigate a surreal world of nightmare in the hopes of finding your memories. This is a mild survival horror experience completed for Dream Jam and inspired by stalker horror games like outlast. You can play the most updated version here or support me further checking out the itch.io details below.





Recollect Memories

Finding the three large memory shards is the key to waking up. Find all of the small memories as an added challenge


Face Nightmares

Defend your self from monsters with a sword or hide in one of the wardrobes..

Wake Up

Use beds to stay sleepy amidst the harrasment of monsters and when the time is ready, use the clock to wake from the dream.
