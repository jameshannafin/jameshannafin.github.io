Game Title: Awesome Game

Overview

This document outlines the design for the game "Awesome Game". It includes details on gameplay mechanics, story, characters, and more.

Gameplay Mechanics

The game will feature a mix of action and puzzle-solving elements. Players will navigate through various levels, each with unique challenges and enemies.

Story

The story follows the protagonist on a quest to save their world from an impending threat. Along the way, they will encounter allies and adversaries, each with their own backstory.

Characters

Key characters include the protagonist, their mentor, and the main antagonist. Each character will have distinct abilities and personalities.
